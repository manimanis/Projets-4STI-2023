Cities of Tunisia

Djerba

Djerba is the largest island in North Africa, known for its beautiful beaches, rich history, and unique culture.

Sousse

Sousse is a coastal city known for its stunning medina, UNESCO World Heritage site, and vibrant nightlife.

Bizerte

Bizerte is a port city with a rich history, featuring a well-preserved old town and a picturesque harbor.

Sidi Bou Said

Sidi Bou Said is a charming blue and white village perched on a cliff overlooking the Mediterranean Sea, known for its picturesque streets and stunning views.

Tozeur

Tozeur is an oasis city located in the Sahara Desert, famous for its palm groves, traditional architecture, and proximity to the Chott el Djerid salt flats.
